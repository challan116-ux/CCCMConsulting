Deep dive

EDI that onboards itself.

SignalEDI replaces months of manual trading-partner setup with an AI-guided onboarding funnel. It ingests partner documents, maps them to X12 transaction sets, runs them through automated validation, and exchanges them over SFTP — all under a multi-tenant, compliance-first platform with billing built in.
